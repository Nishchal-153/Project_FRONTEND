iPhone 14 and 14 Plus Details

Specifications

  • Display: 6.1-inch Liquid Retina display (14) / 6.7-inch Liquid Retina display (14 Plus); 1792 x 828 pixels (14) / 2778 x 1284 pixels (14 Plus); 60Hz refresh rate
  • Chip: A16 Bionic chip with 5-core Neural Engine; 5G capable
  • Camera: Dual 12MP camera system (Ultra Wide and Wide) with Night mode; ProRAW, ProRes video recording; 12MP TrueDepth front camera with Night mode, 4K Dolby Vision HDR recording
  • Battery: Up to 17 hours of talk time (14) / up to 20 hours of talk time (14 Plus); up to 65 hours of audio playback (14) / up to 80 hours of audio playback (14 Plus)
  • Water and Dust Resistance: Rated IP68 (maximum depth of 6 meters up to 30 minutes) under IEC standard 60529
  • Operating System: iOS 16
  • Dimensions: 146.7 x 71.5 x 7.65 mm (14) / 160.8 x 78.1 x 7.65 mm (14 Plus); weight of 171 grams (14) / 204 grams (14 Plus)
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) with 2x2 MIMO, Bluetooth 5.2, NFC with reader mode, Ultra Wideband chip for spatial awareness
  • Sensors: Face ID, Barometer, Three-axis gyro, Accelerometer, Proximity sensor, Ambient light sensor
  • Colors: Black, Blue, Green, Pink, Red, White

Price and Availability

Price: $999 (14) / $1,099 (14 Plus)

Available Quantity: 10

Box Details: Comes in a white box with the Apple logo and includes a USB-C to Lightning cable and 20W USB-C power adapter.
