iPhone 14 Pro and Pro Max Details

Specifications

  • Display: 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display (Pro) / 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display (Pro Max); 2532 x 1170 pixels (Pro) / 2778 x 1284 pixels (Pro Max); 120Hz ProMotion (adaptive refresh rate on Pro models only)
  • Chip: A16 Bionic chip with 5-core Neural Engine; 5G capable
  • Camera: Pro 12MP camera system (Ultra Wide, Wide, and Telephoto) with Night mode; ProRAW, ProRes video recording; 12MP TrueDepth front camera with Night mode, 4K Dolby Vision HDR recording; LiDAR Scanner
  • Battery: Up to 22 hours of talk time (Pro) / up to 28 hours of talk time (Pro Max); up to 75 hours of audio playback (Pro) / up to 95 hours of audio playback (Pro Max)
  • Water and Dust Resistance: Rated IP68 (maximum depth of 6 meters up to 30 minutes) under IEC standard 60529
  • Operating System: iOS 16
  • Dimensions: 146.7 x 71.5 x 7.65 mm (Pro) / 160.8 x 78.1 x 7.65 mm (Pro Max); weight of 204 grams (Pro) / 238 grams (Pro Max)
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) with 2x2 MIMO, Bluetooth 5.2, NFC with reader mode, Ultra Wideband chip for spatial awareness
  • Sensors: Face ID, LiDAR Scanner, Barometer, Three-axis gyro, Accelerometer, Proximity sensor, Ambient light sensor
  • Colors: Graphite, Gold, Silver, Sierra Blue

Price and Availability

Price: $1,199 (Pro) / $1,299 (Pro Max)

Available Quantity: 5

Box Details: Comes in a white box with the Apple logo and includes a USB-C to Lightning cable and 20W USB-C power adapter.
